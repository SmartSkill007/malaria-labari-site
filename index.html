MalariaLabari

WhatsApp Health Bot for Malaria Awareness in Northern Nigeria

MalariaLabari is a community-focused WhatsApp bot that delivers daily malaria prevention tips in Hausa. Our mission is to empower people with timely, relevant, and culturally appropriate health information using natural language AI and voice technologies.

The bot sends Hausa-language voice messages and health advice each day, helping communities stay informed and protected.
